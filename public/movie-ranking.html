The Best Movies According to Cristiana

My Top 3 movies of all-time.

Before Sunrise

This movie is the first movie on a triology called Before and it takes place in 1994 in Vienna.
The movie follows a girl and a boy while they explore the city and get to know each other.
Best Romance movie ever made!

Before Sunset

This movie is the second movie on a triology called Before and it takes place in 2003 in Paris.
The movie follows a woman and a man while they explore the city and talk about the night they met in Vienna.
The plot stays the same but the vibe is immaculated!

Before Midnight

This movie is the third movie on a triology called Before and it takes place in 2011 in Greece.
The movie follows a woman and a man married and with kids, and their marriage problems, even though they're romance started in a epic way and with passion
It's not an happy ever after which makes it realistic!
